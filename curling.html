Curling Mascara

Curling mascara enhances lash curvature while adding definition. Perfect for those looking to create a wide-eyed, curled lash effect without using an eyelash curler.

Popular Brands That Make Curling Mascara:

Benefit Cosmetics – Roller Lash
Maybelline – The Falsies Push Up Drama
Clinique – High Impact Curling Mascara
Lancôme – Hypnôse Doll Lashes
Bobbi Brown – Eye Opening Mascara
Dior – Diorshow Iconic Mascara
YSL – The Curler Mascara

How To Apply:

Step 1: Start at the base of the lashes.
Step 2: Gently twist the wand upward to the tips of the lashes.
Step 3: Apply one to two coats, as this is usually sufficient.

Things To Avoid:

Avoid zigzagging the wand as it can diminish the curling effect.
Do not overload lashes with product to prevent drooping.

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Glyceryl Stearate - Low risk of skin irritation.
Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil - Typically well-tolerated, rare allergies.
Silica - Possible dryness or itching, mainly with prolonged use.
Polymethyl Methacrylate - Low to moderate risk of irritation.
