Liquid Blush

Liquid blush enhances the cheeks with a vibrant splash of color. Its liquid formula provides a natural finish. Due to its form, it can be easily blended with the skin

Popular Brands That Make Liquid Blush:

NARS – Liquid Blush
Benefit – Benetint
Fenty Beauty – Cheeks Out Freestyle Liquid Blush
Milani – Cheek Kiss Liquid Blush
Rare Beauty – Soft Pinch Liquid Blush
Tarte – Watercolor Blush
L'Oréal – True Match Lumi Glow Nude
Stila – Aqua Glow Watercolor Blush

How To Apply:

Step1: Use a blush brush to pick up the powder and tap off the excess powder
Step2: Apply it to your cheeks by sweeping the brush onto the apples if your cheeks
Step3: Blend it properly
Step4: If wanted, add more blush to achieve the desired intensity of the blush

Things To Avoid:

Avoid applying too much blush, applying too much can give off an unnatural look
Avoid skipping blending
Avoid using the wrong brush

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Talc - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation or rash
Mica - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation or itchiness
Silica - Possible Reaction: Dryness or irritation
Magnesium Stearate - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation
