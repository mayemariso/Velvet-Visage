Waterproof Mascara

Waterproof mascara is designed to resist moisture and smudging, ideal for long wear in humid conditions or during physical activities.

Popular Brands That Make Waterproof Mascara:

Maybelline – Great Lash Waterproof Mascara
L'Oréal – Voluminous Waterproof
Too Faced – Better Than Sex Waterproof Mascara
Clinique – High Impact Waterproof Mascara
Dior – Diorshow Waterproof Mascara
Lancôme – Monsieur Big Waterproof
Buxom – Lash Waterproof Mascara

How To Apply:

Step 1: Start at the base of your lashes.
Step 2: Gently sweep the wand up to the tip of the lashes.
Step 3: Apply multiple coats for greater length, allowing each coat to dry before applying the next.

Things To Avoid:

Avoid pumping the wand as it introduces air and dries out the formula.
Avoid applying too many layers quickly, which can lead to clumping.

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Glyceryl Stearate - Low risk of skin irritation.
Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil - Typically well-tolerated, rare allergies.
Silica - Possible dryness or itching, mainly with prolonged use.
Polymethyl Methacrylate - Low to moderate risk of irritation.
