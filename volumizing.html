Volumizing Mascara

Volumizing mascara thickens lashes, providing a fuller and more dramatic look. This type of mascara is ideal for those seeking to amplify the volume of their natural eyelashes for a more pronounced effect.

Popular Brands That Make Volumizing Mascara:

Maybelline – Volum' Express The Colossal
L'Oréal – Voluminous Carbon Black
Too Faced – Better Than Sex Mascara
Benefit Cosmetics – BADgal BANG! Volumizing Mascara
Dior – Diorshow Mascara
Buxom – Lash Volumizing Mascara
Urban Decay – Perversion Mascara

How To Apply:

Step 1: Wiggle the wand at the base of your lashes to deposit more product.
Step 2: Pull the wand through to the tips of your lashes.
Step 3: Apply several layers for maximum volume.
Step 4: Allow each coat to slightly dry before reapplying the next layer.

Things To Avoid:

Do not apply too many layers too quickly to avoid heavy, spider-like lashes.
Avoid using old or expired mascara to prevent clumping and irritation.

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Glyceryl Stearate - Low risk of skin irritation.
Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil - Typically well-tolerated, rare allergies.
Silica - Possible dryness or itching, mainly with prolonged use.
Polymethyl Methacrylate - Low to moderate risk of irritation.
