Powder Foundation

Powder Foundation comes in different skin tone colors that can suit every skin type. It comes in loose or pressed form.

Popular Brands That Make Powder Foundations:

MAC – Studio Fix Powder Plus Foundation
Maybelline – Fit Me Matte + Poreless Powder
BareMinerals – Original Loose Powder Foundation
Fenty Beauty – Pro Filt’r Soft Matte Powder Foundation
L'Oréal – Infallible Fresh Wear Powder Foundation
Estée Lauder – Double Wear Stay-in-Place Powder
Clinique – Almost Powder Makeup

How To Apply:

Step1: Start with a clean and moisturized skin. Apply primer if needed
Step2:Use a brush or a sponge to pick the powder up
Step3: Apply the foundation and blend it properly, Blending from the center of the face outward
Step4: Apply additional layers if needed
Step5: Finish with setting powder or spray for a long lasting wear

Things To Avoid:

Avoid skipping primer. Primer helps the foundation to last longer
Avoid using too much foundation, it can look too thick or cakey
Avoid applying it on dry and flaky skin
Avoid using the wrong shade
Avoid not blending the foundation properly
