Matte Lipstick

Matte lipstick is highly pigmented and gives a bold, non shiny finish. It provides full coverage with a smooth, velvety texture, giving a long lasting wear.

Popular Brands That Make Matte Lipstick:

MAC – Matte Lipstick
Maybelline – SuperStay Matte Ink
NARS – Velvet Matte Lip Pencil
Fenty Beauty – Mattemoiselle Plush Matte Lipstick
Huda Beauty – Power Bullet Matte Lipstick
Anastasia Beverly Hills – Matte Lipstick
Kylie Cosmetics – Matte Lip Kit
Revlon – Ultra HD Matte Lip Color

How To Apply:

Step1: Exfoliate your lips and apply a hydrating lip balm to ensure a smooth application of the lipstick.
Step2: apply the lipstick directly into your lips, either starting from the sides or the middle.
Step3: apply additional layers if desired to have an intense color.
Step4: Gently blot with a tissue for a more natural look

Things To Avoid:

Avoid applying the lipstick when your lips are dry, make sure to moisturize your lips before applying
Avoid applying to many layers to ensure a natural look
Avoid touching your lips too frequently to avoid smothering it all over
Avoid using on a chapped lips

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Beeswax - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation or swelling
Candelilla Wax - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation
Carnauba Wax - Possible Reaction: Mild irritation
Petrolatum - Possible Reaction: Redness or itching
Castor Oil - Possible Reaction: Rash or itching
Lanolin - Possible Reaction: Skin irritation, rash, or itching
