Stick Foundation

Stick foundation is a solid, portable foundation that gives medium to full coverage. It is ideal for a quick application

Popular Brands That Make Stick Foundations:

Maybelline – Fit Me Shine-Free + Balance Stick Foundation
Lancôme – Teint Idole Ultra Wear Foundation Stick
Revlon – PhotoReady Insta-Fix Stick

How To Apply:

Step 1: Start with clean and moisturized skin. Apply primer if needed.
Step 2: Dispense a small amount of liquid foundation onto the sponge.
Step 3: Apply the liquid foundation and blend it properly, blending from the center of the face outward.
Step 4: Apply additional layers if needed.
Step 5: Finish with setting powder or spray for long-lasting wear.

Things To Avoid:

Avoid skipping primer. Primer helps the foundation to last longer
Avoid using too much foundation, it can look too thick or cakey
Avoid applying it on dry and flaky skin
Avoid using the wrong shade
Avoid not blending the foundation properly

Possible Allergies to Get From Chemicals:

Silicones (Dimethicone) - Skin irritation or breakouts
Glycerin - Skin irritation or redness
Titanium Dioxide - Skin irritation or itching
Iron Oxides - Redness, itching, or rash
Zinc Oxide - Itching or redness
Sodium Chloride - Skin irritation or dryness
Talc - Skin irritation or rash
